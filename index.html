Privacy Policy – Stickerbook App

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal or device data. It works completely offline, without analytics, tracking, or internet access.

Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for young children and complies with COPPA and GDPR-K requirements. No personal information is ever collected from children or any other users.

Advertising and Purchases

There are no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no user accounts.

Permissions

The app does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other personal device data.
